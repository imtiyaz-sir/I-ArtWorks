Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

Typical turnaround is 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and artist availability. Rush orders (1-2 weeks) are available for an additional fee.

Can I request revisions?

Yes! Most commissions include 2-3 revision rounds. Your artist will provide progress updates and incorporate your feedback.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We guarantee your satisfaction. If the final artwork doesn't meet your expectations, we'll work with you to make it right or provide a full refund.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! We carefully package and ship artwork worldwide. Shipping is free within India, and international shipping costs are calculated based on location.
